📄 Legal

1. Acceptance

By using CampusMart you agree to these terms. If you disagree, stop using the platform.

2. User Conduct

You must be a registered student or staff. You agree not to post fraudulent, stolen or illegal items.

3. Listings

All listings must be accurate. CampusMart reserves the right to remove any violating listing.

4. Transactions

CampusMart does not process payments. All transactions happen in person at Campus Safe Zones.

5. Safety

Always meet at designated Campus Safe Zones. Never send money in advance.

Data We Collect

We collect your name, email, phone, campus and any info you provide in your profile or listings.

How We Use It

Your data is used to operate the marketplace and send account emails. We never sell your data.

Your Rights

Request deletion by contacting support@campusmart.co.ke. We process requests within 30 days.

What We Store

We use localStorage to remember your login session, theme preference, watchlist and recently viewed items.

No Tracking

We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking cookies.

User Agreement

You must be at least 16 years old. You must not post stolen goods, harass users or create fake accounts.

Limitation of Liability

CampusMart is a peer-to-peer platform. We are not responsible for the quality or safety of listed items.
